 Design Strategy — WiFi Generation
Branch 1 — Baseline

feature/wifi6-baseline

AP3000 / AP4000 fleet. MCS ceiling: 11 (1024-QAM). OFDMA, BSS Color, TWT. No MLO, no 320 MHz, no MCS 12/13. Current-state benchmark for clients already on WiFi 6 hardware.

MCS 0–11 OFDMA BSS Color
Branch 2 — Complete

feature/wifi7-complete

AP5020 / AP5022 / AP5060 fleet. MCS ceiling: 13 (4096-QAM, SNR ≥ 36 dB). MLO (EMLSR/STR), 320 MHz (6 GHz), Multi-AP Coordination, Preamble Puncturing, HARQ. Full WiFi 7 capability.

WiFi 6 — AP3000

AP3000

AP4000
