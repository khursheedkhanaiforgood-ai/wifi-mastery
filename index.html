5G ↔ WiFi Teaching Bridge — Selected Mappings (HLD Section 8)
WiFi Concept5G EquivalentKey Difference
MLO (WiFi 7)Carrier AggregationMLO is AP+client negotiated; CA is network-assigned. Both aggregate bands simultaneously.
MCS 0–13 (802.11be)NR MCS 0–28MCS 13 = 4096-QAM, WiFi 7 only; SNR ≥ 38 dB. WiFi ceiling is lower than NR.
OFDMA schedulergNB schedulerWiFi OFDMA layered on CSMA/CA — contention still exists.
XIQ AutoRF (DCS+TPC)SON (ANR+CCO+MLB)SON is centralized+predictive; AutoRF is per-AP+reactive. 30-min cycle.
BSS ColoringICIC / eICICBoth reduce inter-cell interference. BSS Color is per-frame header; ICIC is scheduling.
Multi-AP CoordinationCoMP / JT-CoMPBoth coordinate multi-cell TX. WiFi 7 Multi-AP Coord still ratifying (TGbe).
Airtime % per radioPRB utilization %Same concept: shared medium utilization. Rev Wi-Fi: Airtime = App_Kbps / Device_Kbps.
DFS channels (5 GHz)Licensed spectrumDFS is detect-and-vacate; 5G has licensed priority. DFS adds 60-s CAC delay.
